Rental Agreement


Acceptance of Terms and Conditions

Your use of this website constitutes your agreement to be legally bound by the Terms and Conditions outlined herein. This is your Rental Agreement.


Rental Rates and Terms

  • Rental rates are subject to change until your reservation is confirmed.
  • Full payment, including daily rate(s), any applicable taxes and fees, etc., is due upon booking.
  • You will receive a confirmation email after full payment is made that will contain information pertaining to your check in.
  • CANCELLATION: You have the option to cancel your reservation without penalty up to 14 days before your stay. If you decide to cancel your reservation within 14 days of your scheduled stay, you will incur a 50% cancellation fee based on the total cost of your reservation.
  • Dates are non-transferable.
  • There are no refunds for early departure.


Taxes and Fees

Taxes imposed on the Total Rental Amount:

  • 6% KY Sales and Use Tax
  • 3% Grant County Transient Tax
  • 1% KY State Transient Tax
  • 3% Credit Card Processing Fee

Cleaning Fees are as follows:

  • Flat 1-2 Adults/Children = $50 per stay
  • 3-4 Adults/Children = $70 per stay


Security Deposit

We reserve the right to charge the credit or debit card you used to make your original reservation for any damages that may have occurred during the rental period or for any items that are found to be missing at the end of the rental period. This charge may occur up to 7 business days after Check-Out and without any prior notice.


Payment

Payment will be accepted via credit or debit card through the website.


Self Check-In

  • Check-In time starts at 4:00 p.m. (EST) to ensure adequate time for housekeeping duties.
  • An early Check-In time should not be expected.
  • To ensure that any cleanliness or damage issues are promptly addressed, please report them within 24 hours of your arrival. If no concerns are reported within this period, we will assume that the property is in acceptable condition.


Self Check-Out

  • Check-Out is no later than 11:00 a.m. EST.
  • A late Check-Out should not be expected


Guest Required Duties

Kitchen:

  • Wash, dry, and put away all dishes used during your stay. Clean dishes are allowed to be left in the rack to dry at the end of your stay.
  • Remove all food items from the refrigerator and cabinets and dispose of them properly before checking out.

Trash Removal:

Gather all trash from all inside trash cans and place it in the trash can outside in the parking area.


Furniture:

Please make sure all furniture is returned to the position it was in when you first arrived.


Doors:

Secure and lock all doors upon departure.

Towels:

Please place all used towels in the bathtub at the end of your stay.

Bedding:

You are not required to remove/wash any bedding upon departure; however, we have provided a stain stick to use in case any unexpected stains occur on bedding, towels, etc. Items with excessive soiling should be treated and washed. We appreciate your help in keeping our place clean and sanitary for all guests.

We advise that you keep your vehicles locked at all times and ensure all doors and windows are secured and locked whenever you are away from the property.

Vehicles and Parking

Free Parking is available on the parking pad behind the property. Please respect the neighbors and refrain from blocking the gravel entryway from the street. There is no on-street parking within the City of Dry Ridge.

Evidence of any animal(s) in the home will result in an additional $250 cleaning fee. Please be honest and refrain from sneaking pets onto the property. If you do need to board your pets, there are boarding facilities nearby.

  • We have a strict no smoking policy inside the home. Any smoking inside the property will result in a minimum charge of $200. If you choose to smoke outside, we kindly request that you pick up and dispose of any cigarette trash.
