Our Story

Delivering for America...and running an Airbnb we hope you'll like!

Justin and Nikki Baker - Owners of The Staywell

Welcome to The Staywell!


As a hardworking couple, we understand the value of a great stay after a long day. We take great pride in our work as public servants, and we bring that same level of dedication to our one and only Airbnb -- The Staywell. We have put alot of thought and effort into creating a space that feels like home to our guests. Whether you're in town for business or leisure, we've designed our rental to meet all of your needs. We're committed to providing the best possible experience for our guests, and we hope you feel that from the moment you arrive. We are open to any suggestions, questions, or comments you may have about our rental and encourage you to reach out to us through our Contact Us page.


Thank you for choosing The Staywell!
